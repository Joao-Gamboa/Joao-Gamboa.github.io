Mobility Operational Services

Who are we?

At MOS, we are dedicated to improving the lives of physically handicapped individuals by addressing one of their most significant challenges: navigating stairs. Our team is passionate about developing innovative solutions that enhance mobility and independence for wheelchair users.

The Three-Wheel Attachable Mechanism

This innovative device is designed to be easily attached to any wheelchair, allowing a second person to pull the chair up the stairs step by step. By distributing the weight and leveraging mechanical advantage, this mechanism aims to eliminate the physical strain and risk traditionally associated with carrying both the individual and the wheelchair separately.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create practical and effective solutions that empower physically handicapped individuals, providing them with the freedom to move more easily and safely in their daily lives. We are committed to rigorous research and development, focusing on user-friendly and accessible designs.
