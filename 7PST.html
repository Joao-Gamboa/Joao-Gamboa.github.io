Thermal Systems Design

Aveiro PST Factory

In the thermal project in Aveiro, the design of an industrial cooling network for continuous 24-hour operation requires meticulous planning. Key constraints involve integrating renewable energy sources, maintaining uninterrupted operation, and preventing condensation outside piping. To address these challenges, the project emphasizes the incorporation of redundant equipment to ensure seamless performance and reliability, even in the event of potential failures.

My Main Role

In the Aveiro thermal project, I led dimensioning and component selection, focusing on piping insulation and integrating renewable energy sources like solar. Engaging with partners and suppliers, I fostered strong relationships to support project goals. Additionally, I aided team members, encouraged cross-communication, and drove continuous improvement, fostering a culture of enhancement, sustainability, and efficiency.
