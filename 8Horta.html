Verde ao Cubo

What is Verde ao Cubo

Verde ao Cubo is a project aimed at improving children's dietary habits by introducing them to healthy eating through small-scale organic cultivation. By fostering a connection between children and their food through hands-on cultivation experiences, the project promotes responsible nutrition and environmental sustainability from an early age.

My Main Role

As the mechanical engineer of the Verde ao Cubo project, my primary role involved guiding the design process to ensure manufacturability and functionality. I defined working conditions and conducted FEA analysis simulations, including buckling analysis, to optimize performance and durability while considering factors such as draft angles and constant thickness. Additionally, I played a key role in creating pitches and Virtual Reality videos to effectively showcase the product's features and benefits.

Real Life Prototype

The 3D-printed real-life prototype of Verde ao Cubo brought our design to life, functioning as intended and allowing us to test its working concept. This physical model provided valuable validation of our ideas and paved the way for further development.
