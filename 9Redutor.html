Gearbox system with parallel shafts

Engineering Process

This project entails the comprehensive design and analysis of a gearbox system. It involves crucial stages like gear dimensioning, bearing sizing, and fatigue calculation, focusing on critical components such as shafts and keyways. Through rigorous analysis and synthesis, it aims to deliver a robust and efficient gearbox solution tailored to specific operational requirements.

Documentation

Crafted detailed cinematic representations and conducted thorough dimensioning of gears, bearings, and the carter. To ensure optimal performance of the system, we conducted a comprehensive analysis including FEA, fatigue assessment, and evaluation of both yield strength and stiffness. Furthermore, all mechanical drawings were provided to enhance understanding of the components and their functionalities.
